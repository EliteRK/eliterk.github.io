Together with fat, protein and carbohydrates, it is now proven that your body also gets a lot af plastic waste at every single meal due to plastic pollution. But how do you visualize something you can not see? How do you visualize microplastic?

PLASTIC NOT SO FANTASTIC.

Plastic beads! They are tiny, we all know them and they symbolize something we certainly do not want to stuff in our mouths + they are fun to work with. So let's stop the plastic polution before we eat way to many "plastic beads". Bon appetite!
